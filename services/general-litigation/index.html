General Litigation

People and companies may need to appear before a wide variety of courts and tribunals either to pursue relief that is due to them or to defend against claims being made against them. This can include anything from appealing a Court Order to appearing before the BC Ombudsperson. Litigation is a core component of our practice and we have decades of experience with

  • General lawsuits at all levels of Court
  • ICBC claims
  • Negotiations and mediations
  • The BC Residential Tenancy Branch
  • Worksafe BC applications and WCAT Appeals
  • The BC Human Rights Tribunal
