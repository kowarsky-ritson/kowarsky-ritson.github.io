Family Law

Family law has undergone significant changes in the last few years and your rights and obligations regarding partners and families may not be what you expect. We can advise you on these matters at any stage, including:

  • Cohabitation, marriage and separation agreements
  • Resolution of family disputes
  • Divorce, children, division of assets and debts, spousal and child support
  • Litigation
