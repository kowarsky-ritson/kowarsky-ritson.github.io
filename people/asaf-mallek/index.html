Asaf Mallek

Associate Lawyer

  • 604.683.6875 ext. 230
  • asaf@kowarsky.ca

Asaf has a wide-ranging litigation practice and has appeared on behalf of his clients before the BC Court of Appeal, the BC Supreme Court, Bankruptcy Court, the BC Provincial Court, Workers’ Compensation Appeal Tribunal (WCAT), WorkSafeBC, and the BC Human Rights Tribunal. He has experience in a wide range of legal matters ranging from commercial litigation to family law to administrative and tribunal law.

He received a Master of Laws with Commendation from City, University of London (2011), a Bachelor of Laws with Honours from Cardiff University (2009), and a Bachelor of Arts from University of British Columbia (2000).

Asaf was called or admitted as a lawyer in British Columbia (2016), England and Wales (2013), and the State of New York (2011).

His interests include cooking and assorted adventures with his wife and child.
