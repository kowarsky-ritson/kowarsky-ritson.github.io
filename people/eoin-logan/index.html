Eoin Logan

Associate Lawyer

  • 604.683.6875 ext. 231
  • eoin@kowarsky.ca

Eoin joined Kowarsky Ritson LLP in 2015 and has a burgeoning litigation practice. He has represented clients at the Supreme Court of British Columbia and Provincial Courts all over the Lower Mainland. He currently works in various areas of law including of collections, foreclosure, contract litigation, general civil litigation, commercial disputes and family law

Eoin was called to the bar in 2017 having completed his articles with the firm. Eoin completed his law degree and LLM at UCC in Cork, Ireland before completing the NCA exams to be called to the bar in British Columbia.

Eoin’s interests include traveling, watching sports and soccer. He has season tickets for the Vancouver Whitecaps, in the Premier League his team is Tottenham Hotspurs and he plays soccer locally in the Vancouver Metro League. At the weekends he can be found either hiking or snowboarding on the local mountains.
